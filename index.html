It moistens the air

Humidifiers moistens the indoor air. Moistened air helps ease skin and breathing symptoms caused by dry air. Adding in drops of essential oil spreads its scent while maintaining the oils' quality and effectiveness.

Babo charges wirelessly.

Flip the base around and flip the switch to turn off the electromagnet and turn on the wireless charging module. Simply put Babo on the base and a lit LED will show that it is currently charging. The humidifier has an inbuilt battery charged with the wireless technology.
